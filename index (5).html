Who We Are

The startup advisor with an operator's instinct.

Most advisors give you a deck and a handshake. Nob Ventures gives you a partner who's been in the room—building revenue engines, negotiating partnerships, and pressure-testing strategy from the inside out.

Founded by Hilary Malecha out of Cambridge's most prolific startup ecosystem, Nob Ventures works shoulder-to-shoulder with founders at the critical inflection points where good companies become great ones.

We bring the pattern recognition of a venture-backed operator, the relationships of a decade in the Boston tech community, and the urgency of a founder who understands that time is your scarcest resource.
